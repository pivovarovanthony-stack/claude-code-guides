Positioning

SF Agency positions itself as the anti-agency agency. We don't sell marketing services — we educate first, prove competence through content, and only work with clients who come to us. Our education-based funnel model (the Andromeda System) teaches before it sells, creating informed buyers who already trust the methodology before the first call.

Competitive Differentiation

What we are

  • + Education-first, not pitch-first
  • + Data-driven with transparent reporting
  • + Specialized in Meta Ads ecosystem
  • + UAE market expertise
  • + High-ticket, high-value engagements only

What we are not

  • - Not a cheap social media management shop
  • - Not a generalist "we do everything" agency
  • - Not chasing small retainers or pilot projects
  • - Not promising overnight results
  • - Not outsourcing to juniors behind the scenes

Copy Rules
Do
  • Use specific numbers: "3.2x ROAS" not "great results"
  • Lead with the insight, not the setup
  • Write at a 9th-grade reading level
  • Use "you/your" more than "we/our"
  • One idea per sentence
Don't
  • Use words like "synergy," "leverage," "holistic"
  • Make claims without evidence or context
  • Hedge with "maybe," "perhaps," "it depends"
  • Use passive voice
  • Write paragraphs longer than 3 sentences

Photography & Imagery

Visual direction for all photography, illustrations, and image assets used across the brand.

Lighting

Clean, even, professional studio lighting. Soft shadows. No harsh directional light. Slight cool-tone bias to match the blue brand palette. Window light for portrait shots.

Color Grading

Slightly desaturated, cool-neutral tone. Lift the blacks slightly for a modern flat look. Whites should be clean, not warm. Accent colors (blue, cyan) can be pushed in post.

Composition

Clean backgrounds. Ample negative space. Subject off-center (rule of thirds). Tight crops for headshots. Wide shots for environment/context. No clutter.

Subject Matter

Business professionals in modern settings. Clean workspaces, screens showing data/dashboards. Hands typing, meetings, whiteboard sessions. No generic stock handshakes.

Avatar Treatment

Circular crop. Neutral or dark background. Professional headshot. Applied accent ring (cyan on dark, purple on light). Minimum 400x400px source.

3D & Abstract

Chrome/metallic 3D shapes for carousel cover slides. Abstract geometric illustrations for data visualization. Gradient blobs for background texture (blur style).

Mood Keywords

Modern, clean, tech-forward, professional, high-contrast, minimal, precise, data-rich, geometric, sharp, trustworthy, premium. Think: Stripe's design sensibility applied to a performance marketing agency.
